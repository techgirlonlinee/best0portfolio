BRICK is a photography studio that embraces minimalism while maintaining a human touch. Its identity balances clean, structured design with a bit of imperfection, making the brand feel both polished and personal. To achieve this, BRICK’s visual style pairs a sharp, editorial look with a handmade element, capturing the mix of precision and spontaneity that defines the studio.

Brand Identity

2023

The hand-painted “C” is the standout feature, adding a raw, expressive detail to the otherwise refined logo. Created through brushstroke experimentation and digital tweaking, it was carefully chosen to feel natural yet intentional. This small imperfection brings warmth to the brand, making it feel more personal and less rigid.

The art direction relies on a sophisticated blend of typography and color. Victor Narrow, a high-contrast serif, conveys elegance, while GT Flexa Mono adds a structured, utilitarian edge. A neutral palette of off-white, silver, and deep black provides a timeless foundation that lets the photography shine. This interplay between refined type and restrained colors creates a cohesive identity that feels modern yet warm, free from excessive digital polish.
