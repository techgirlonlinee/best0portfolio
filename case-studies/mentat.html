Mentat is an AI-driven protocol built for blockchain ecosystems, providing real-time, accurate, and context-aware insights. Inspired by the Mentats in Dune—humans trained to think with machine-like precision—it brings structured intelligence to technical guidance, market analysis, and dApp navigation, making blockchain information more accessible and actionable.

Brand Design

2025

The logo system reflects the balance between human intelligence and machine logic. Built on a geometric grid inspired by classic technical drawings, it conveys structure and precision, while subtle blueprint tones and soft detailing add a human touch. This captures Mentat’s role as an advanced AI with machine accuracy and human-like intuition – balancing analytical rigor with approachability.

Designed an airdrop claiming flow that had to account for two types of users: those from an EVM chain and those from a non-EVM chain, each requiring a different number of steps. The challenge was to streamline the experience while handling errors and filtering out non-eligible claimers without breaking the flow. The design balances clarity with high-energy visuals to keep the experience both seamless and hype-driven.

Warm neutrals create a grounded, organic foundation, while subtle contrasts add a modern, approachable feel. The palette blends timeless desert tones with a tech-forward edge, reinforcing Mentat’s dual nature—both advanced and human-centric.

NB International was chosen for its clean, contemporary look that reflects this balance. Its open letterforms ensure readability, while the geometric structure echoes Mentat’s methodical, complex reasoning.

Pixelated silhouettes reflect Mentat’s Dune-inspired roots, merging human insight with digital intelligence. The desert setting ties into the brand’s earthy palette and iconic landscape, while pixelation references classic computing. The lone figure in the vast desert symbolizes self-sufficiency and deep knowledge, reinforcing Mentat’s role as an advanced AI with a human perspective.
