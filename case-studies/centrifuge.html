Centrifuge is a decentralized platform bridging real-world assets like real estate and structured credit into decentralized finance (DeFi) through asset tokenization. It enables investors to transparently pool funds, providing easier access to traditionally illiquid markets. Its dashboards emphasize clean, minimal, and intuitive design, highlighting key financial data—such as total value locked (TVL), APR, asset types, and liquidity statuses—supported by detailed visualizations, including line graphs and bar charts.

These visualizations simplify tracking of investments and pool performance, clearly differentiating investment pools by asset type and risk level, facilitating quick and informed decisions. Wallet and user flows support multiple users, integrating comprehensive KYC processes and proxied accounts, visually clarifying roles, permissions, and compliance. Strategic use of whitespace, clear typography, and consistent color coding (blues, yellows, greys) enhance readability and reinforce a trustworthy fintech aesthetic.
