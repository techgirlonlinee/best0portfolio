The logo reflects probabilistic systems, distributed networks, and structured randomness. Inspired by random walk diagrams, the process began by sketching out scattered dots representing decentralized, non-deterministic movement, evolving into a minimalist, soft, organic cluster. The final logo balances abstraction with structure, maintaining a polished, data-driven feel that bridges crypto and traditional finance.

Beige is the primary surface color, creating a soft, neutral base that feels modern and approachable. Green ties into the finance and tech space, offering familiarity, while neon green adds a distinct, forward-thinking edge for those in the crypto world. Two sans-serif fonts are used—one for titles to add character while staying accessible, and another for text to ensure readability.
