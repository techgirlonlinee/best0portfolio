Unit A is a development studio built on collaboration between African and European talent, working across blockchain, AI, and front-end technologies. The website was designed to reflect this mission with clarity and precision—using bold typography, a strong grid, and editorial pacing to communicate the studio’s technical focus and social purpose. The result is a confident, utilitarian site that highlights delivery and impact.

Art Direction & Web Design

2023

The design leans into typographic hierarchy, modular layout, and a restrained monochrome palette. Sharp sans-serifs and generous spacing bring structure, while subtle shifts in rhythm introduce just enough softness. This balance supports a clear, efficient experience—aligned with Unit A’s identity as a development-focused, impact-driven studio.
